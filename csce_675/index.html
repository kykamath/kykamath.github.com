Howdy!

I am a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas A&M University, College Station. I work in the infolab with Prof. James Caverlee.

My research interests are in the area of social and information network analysis. I am in particular interested in studying and developing efficient algorithms for real-time communitiy detection on these networks. I am interested in learning how social media fits into the paradigm of digital libraries. I want to learn if I can learn concepts in this course that I can use in my research. During my previous semesters I have taken courses related to digital libraries like, Hypertext, Computer-Human Interaction, Information Retrieval, etc.
